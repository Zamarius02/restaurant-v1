The HM Desert Lounge is a local blacked owned business that is known for serving high class society. HM is Memphis's only dessert and novelty gathering place. We serve amazing original flavored chicken wings, deserts, waffles, and several delicious sides to complement your entree. Great for dining by yourself or with friends ! Either way you will enjoy quality food along with quality service.
